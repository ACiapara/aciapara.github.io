Business Analytics Capstone Presentation

As a culmination of my Wharton Business Analytics specialization on Coursera, I undertook a comprehensive capstone project designed to bridge theoretical knowledge with real-world application. This project challenged me to analyze a complex dataset, spanning customer, operations, HR, and accounting analytics, to formulate strategic business recommendations for a pressing industry challenge. The following PowerPoint presentation visually outlines my analytical process and data-driven insights, demonstrating my ability to translate data into actionable business strategies.
The capstone is written as though I were a part of YAHOO!'s DATA team, and includes "Application Exercises" which were required components to show my understanding of business problem solving and decision making.
