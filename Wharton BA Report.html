Business Analytics Capstone Report

As a culmination of my Wharton Business Analytics specialization on Coursera, I undertook a comprehensive capstone project designed to bridge theoretical knowledge with real-world application. This project challenged me to analyze a complex dataset, spanning customer, operations, HR, and accounting analytics, to formulate strategic business recommendations for a pressing industry challenge. The following written report delves into the detailed analytical process, data-driven insights, and strategic recommendations, demonstrating my ability to translate data into actionable business strategies with clear, concise, and impactful communication.
The capstone is written as though I were a part of YAHOO!'s DATA team to show my understanding of business problem solving and decision making.
