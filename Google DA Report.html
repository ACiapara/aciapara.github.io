Data Analytics Capstone

The Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate provided me with a comprehensive foundation in data analysis, encompassing eight rigorous courses and practical assessments. This capstone project serves as a testament to my ability to apply these skills to real-world scenarios. It showcases my proficiency in the entire data analysis lifecycle, from understanding stakeholder needs and data wrangling to utilizing advanced tools like SQL, R (Tidyverse, ggPlot2, dplyr, tidyr, tibble), Tableau, and Excel to visualize findings and deliver actionable insights. This project demonstrates not only my technical expertise but also my ability to effectively communicate data-driven recommendations.
The capstone is written as though I were a part of Divvy's data team. Divvy is the city of Chicago's bikeshare program, operated by Lyft. The data analyzed is real and provided by Divvy.
